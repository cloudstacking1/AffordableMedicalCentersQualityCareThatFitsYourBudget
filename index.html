    What Is a Medical Center?

    A medical center is a facility that offers a broad range of healthcare services. Unlike standalone clinics or hospitals, medical centers often include multiple departments such as primary care, diagnostics, specialty treatments, rehabilitation, and urgent care—all under one roof. The primary advantage of choosing a medical center near you is accessibility to comprehensive services that can address various health concerns without having to visit multiple locations.
